Worlds 5 tallest mountains

Mount Everest

Mount everest is the tallest mountain in the world coming in at 29,029 feet, it is also the most climbed mountain in the world with 266 people attempting the climb, around 145 were successful while 121 have failed. It was first climbed in 1953. Mount everest is the parent mountain of all the other mountains in the top 5.

K2

K2 is the worlds 2nd tallest mountain coming in at 28,251 feet tall. It has a pretty even successful to failure ratio with 45 successful attempts to scale the mountain with 44 people coming short. The first ever climb of the mountain was done in 1954.

Kangchenjunga

Kangchenjunga is the 3rd tallest mountain in the world, its height is 28,169 feet and it was first climed in 1955, just a year after k2 was conquered.

Lhotse

Lhotse is the 4th tallest mountain in the world, its height is 27,940 feet and it was first climbed in 1956 the latest of the 5 mountains on the list. It has an even pass to fail ratio of 26 to 26.

Makalu

Last but certainly not least we have Makalu. Makalu's height comes in at 27,838 and itwas first climbed in 1955 which was the same year Kangchenjunga was first climed.
